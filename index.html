Hi, I'm Roshana. I design motion and edit video for brands, artists, and storytellers across film, social, and broadcast.

My work spans narrative editing, color grading, sound design, and motion graphics. I bring a cinematic eye to every cut, grounded in pacing, emotion, and craft.

I work effectively both in teams and independently, leading or delivering end to end as needed. From long-form documentaries to focused social cuts, I adapt quickly and collaborate closely to provide edit support that fits.
